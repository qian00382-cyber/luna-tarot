Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Luna ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Luna AI Tarot Card Reading application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Luna collects minimal information to provide you with personalized tarot readings. This includes:

Information you provide voluntarily: Your name (optional), zodiac sign (optional), and the questions or intentions you share during a reading session.

Usage data: We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking data through our own systems.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to generate personalized AI tarot readings. Questions you submit are sent to our AI provider (Moonshot AI / Kimi) to generate responses. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Reading history, your name, and zodiac preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not maintain a server-side database of your personal readings or account information.

4. AI Processing

Questions and card information are transmitted to our AI API (api.moonshot.cn) to generate readings. Please do not include sensitive personal information such as financial details, passwords, or government identification numbers in your questions.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store and Apple's payment infrastructure. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All billing is handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy practices and terms of service.

6. Third-Party Services

Luna uses the following third-party services:

Moonshot AI (api.moonshot.cn): For generating AI tarot readings. Your question and selected card information is sent to this service.

Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchase processing.

Cloudflare: For content delivery and website hosting.

7. Children's Privacy

Luna is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the information transmitted through our App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Since reading history and preferences are stored locally on your device, you can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App. For any questions about data we may hold, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
