Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Luna ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Luna Tarot application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with a personalized experience:

  • Name (optional) — entered during onboarding to personalize your readings
  • Zodiac sign (optional) — used to personalize your daily card readings
  • Reading history — stored locally on your device only
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics to improve app performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your tarot readings based on your zodiac sign
  • To display your reading history within the app

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

All reading history and personal preferences (name, zodiac sign) are stored locally on your device using browser/app local storage. We do not store your personal readings on our servers.

4. Third-Party AI Services

Luna uses AI services to generate tarot readings. When you request a reading, the card drawn and your question (if provided) are sent to our AI provider to generate a response. No personally identifying information is included in these requests.

5. Children's Privacy

Luna is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your reading history at any time within the app
  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about any data we hold about you

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
